About Us

Welcome to our Movie/Game Review app! We provide an easy-to-use platform where users can share their thoughts, feedback, and experiences on movies and games. Our goal is to help users make informed decisions before watching movies or playing games.

Our Purpose

Our app aims to be a one-stop destination for movie and game enthusiasts. Whether you want to read user reviews, submit your own ratings, or discover new titles, we provide a user-friendly interface to make the process simple and enjoyable.

Features

  • User Reviews: Share your personal reviews and ratings for movies or games.
  • Rating System: Rate movies or games from 1 to 5 stars based on your experience.
  • Search and Filter: Find movies or games based on genre, ratings, or popularity.
  • Submit Feedback: Provide suggestions and feedback directly to the app developers.

Technologies We Use

We use the latest technologies to provide a seamless experience. Our tech stack includes:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (React or Vanilla JS)
  • Backend: Flask (Python) with SQLAlchemy for database management
  • Database: SqlLite (for storing user data, reviews, ratings)
  • Deployment: Hosted on Local System
